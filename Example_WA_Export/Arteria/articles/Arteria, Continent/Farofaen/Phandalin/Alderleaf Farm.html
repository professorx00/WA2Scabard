ALDERLEAF FARM

Cale and Raymond Alderleaf live in the farm north of Phandalin, they have been able to keep the blight at the edges of their farm by grazing their field primarily with goats and some pigs, if the players take too long, or come back they find that the goats have gotten sick and are starting to die.

Quest: Reidoth the Druid. Qelline is a longtime friend of a druid named Reidoth. If she figures out that the characters are looking for specific sites in the area, such as the Kobold Hideout, The Dragon's Cave, or Agatha's Lair, she suggests that they visit Reidoth and ask for his help, "since there's not an inch of the land he doesn't know." She tells the characters that Reidoth recently set out for the ruins of a town called Thundertree, just west of the Neverwinter Wood. The ruins are about fifty miles northwest of Phandalin, and she provides directions so the characters can easily find the place. If the party pursues this quest, see "Ruins of Thundertree" (page 30).



Carp's Story. Qelline's son, Carp, is a spirited and precocious human lad of ten years. He is enchanted by the idea of being an adventurer and says that he was playing in the woods near Tresendar Manor when he found a secret tunnel in a thicket. A couple of "big ugly lizards" came out of the tunnel when he was there. They didn't see him, but it was close. He can take the characters to the tunnel or provide them with directions to the location.
