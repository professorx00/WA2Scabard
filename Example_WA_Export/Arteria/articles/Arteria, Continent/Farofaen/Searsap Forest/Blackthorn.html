A city in a dark forest of searsap and conifer trees, not much use for the aboveboard business of Farofaen.

Government

The Blackthorn family has run the town for hundreds of years.

Industry & Trade

It's importance is largely as a waystation(?) resting point for caravans going to and from Arborport carrying goods and lumber. It was thought to be another base for forestry operations but suitable timber was not found.

Guilds and Factions

Thieves Guild, Forestry Guild, Temple of Mystra

Natural Resources
