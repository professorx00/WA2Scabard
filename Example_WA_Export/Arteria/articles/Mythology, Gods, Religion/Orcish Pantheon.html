Gruumsh, god of rage, war, vision, paranoia. Bahgtru, god of pure, physical strength, patron of the average orc, awesomely strong, utterly oafish "the leg breaker", son of luthic and gruumsh Ilneval, god of warfare, patron of orc captains, lead the charge, overwhelming numbers "the horde leader, the war maker" Luthic, goddess of caves, holds orcs together, healing, home, female orcs. "the cave mother"
 
  “An orc life is a god-ridden life. Luthic's at birth, Luthic's at death, and striving to prove themselves to Gruumsh in between.” — Elminster's notes in Volo's Guide to Monsters.
  Luthic and Ilneval primarily hold sway over the orcs of Ashdrift, before their descent into Avernus. The Orcs in their desperation turn back to gruumsh to help save them from a perilous fate.
