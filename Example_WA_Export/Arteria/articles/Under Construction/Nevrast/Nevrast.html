A mostly equatorial continent, the eastern half ringed with mountains and a chain of volcanoes. The approach to Nevrast by sea is made complicated by many small islands and treacherous reefs. Few have laid eyes on the interior and lived to tell the tale.

Geography

Ringed by mountains and volcanoes, dominated by a large valley.

Ecosystem

Jungle, lots of water flowing to the center of the island.
